## 1. Foundry Dues - The Membership

The core platform subscription. One tier. Democratic. Identical access regardless of archetype or lane.

| Plan | Price | Flavor name | What it includes |
|---|---:|---|---|
| Foundry Dues - Monthly | **$9.99/month** | - | Full platform access, Drafting Table workspaces, intro requests, basic verification, Armory member discounts |
| Foundry Dues - Annual | **$99/year** | "The Long Run" | All of the above + about $20 saved annually, about 2 months free, plus Founder badge for first 500 annual subscribers if approved in launch copy |

**Hard rules (Constitutional):**

- No tiered membership. No "Pro," no "Enterprise," no "Founder" lifetime tier beyond the first-500 cohort marketing layer.
- No lane-priced membership. Spark, Builder, Worker, Operator, Backer, Connector all pay the same.
- Annual prepay is non-refundable past 14 days from start of any new billing cycle.
- Monthly cancellation takes effect end of current billing period.

## 2. The Armory - Templates, SOPs, Checklists, Kits

First-party one-time-purchase documents. Built and reviewed by Werkles, or counsel where legal. Carries the locked compliance disclaimer: "Starter language only. Not legal advice. Engage independent counsel before signing."

### 2.1 Pricing anchors

| Tier | Price | Examples |
|---|---:|---|
| Simple - 1-3 pages | **$9.99** | Single SOP, single checklist, single form, partnership role matrix |
| Mid - 5-15 pages | **$19** | Capital contribution worksheet, first meeting checklist, vendor selection guide |
| Deep / Starter Kit | **$49** | LLC operating agreement skeleton, trade-specific launch checklist, partnership term-sheet kit |
| Werkles signature tool | **$29** | The Bare-Metal Estimator |
| Bundle | **$99** | "First 90 Days Kit" - 5-7 templates bundled. Same price as annual Foundry Dues. |

### 2.2 Member discount rules

| Armory tier | Non-member | Foundry Dues member |
|---|---:|---:|
| Simple ($9.99) | $9.99 | **Free** (unlimited download) |
| Mid ($19) | $19 | **Free** (unlimited download) |
| Werkles signature tool ($29) | $29 | **30% off** ($20.30) |
| Deep / Starter Kit ($49) | $49 | **30% off** ($34.30) |
| Bundle ($99) | $99 | **Free** (unlimited download) |

Rationale: members get unlimited access to Simple, Mid, and Bundle items. Werkles' headline tools and deep legal-adjacent kits keep a member discount but are never free. They reflect real attorney-review value and should remain financially meaningful to members.

### 2.3 Legal compliance rules

- Every legal-adjacent template (LLC, partnership agreement, term sheet, operating agreement) requires attorney review **before sale**.
- Every template carries the disclaimer: *"Starter language only. Not legal advice. Engage independent counsel before signing."*
- Werkles never represents that any template is jurisdiction-specific without attorney sign-off on the specific jurisdiction.
- Member discount applies to the base price only; sales tax, where applicable, is added to the discounted price.

## 3. The Crucible - Verification Services

Third-party verification routed through Werkles. Costs are passed through transparently. **Werkles is not a profit center on trust.** Maximum markup is a $5 flat handling fee per check, no exceptions.

### 3.1 Verification pricing

| Verification | Price to user | Werkles take | Notes |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| Identity (Stripe Identity, ID + selfie) | **$0** (bundled in Foundry Dues, 1/year/member) | -$1.50 estimated | Free for Foundry members |
| Identity re-verification (after 30-day staleness or document expiration) | **$2.99** | about $1.50 margin | Cost recovery |
| Phone (Twilio Verify, SMS or voice) | **$0** (bundled in Foundry Dues, ID + phone together) | about -$0.05 | Free with membership |
| Funds (Plaid Assets, bank snapshot) | **$9.99** | $0-$7 margin depending on Plaid Item pricing | Backer requirement before applying to Capital seats |
| Funds re-verification | First/year **free** (member), subsequent **$2.99** | At cost | Cost recovery |
| License (state-board lookup, where automated) | **$14.99 per state per check** | about $5 handling | Operator lane, varies by state |
| Reference (Checkr) | **$14.99** | $5 handling | Checkr's $10 + $5 |
| Employment (Checkr, passthrough by years) | **passthrough + $5** | $5 handling | Vendor cost + handling |
| Background - Basic (Checkr) | **$34.99** | $5 handling | Checkr's $29.99 + $5. SSN trace, sex offender, watchlist, national criminal. |
| Background - Essential (Checkr) | **$59.99** | $5 handling | Checkr's $54.99 + $5. Adds county criminal. |
| Background - Complete (Checkr) | **$94.99** | $5 handling | Checkr's $89.99 + $5. Adds state + federal criminal. |
| Continuous monitoring (Checkr) | **$2.99/month per individual** | about $1.29 margin | Checkr's $1.70/mo + handling |

### 3.2 Verification rules (Constitutional)

- **Membership unlocks access to verification providers.** It does not unlock verification itself - that depends on the credentials the user actually has. Werkles cannot make anyone trustworthy. We just make claims harder to fake.
- **Per-check costs are published transparently on the Pricing page.** No hidden markups. Members can audit Werkles' margin per check.
- **Background checks are user-initiated, user-paid, and FCRA-compliant.** The user is the consumer of their own report. They choose to display the resulting badge.
- **No verification gates on Free-tier users initiating verification on themselves.** A non-member can pay for a background check on themselves and Werkles will route it through Checkr. They cannot intro to Heavyweight-verified members until they themselves are verified per the v0.3 access weight system.
- **Foundry Dues subsidizes only the bundled identity + phone verification (1/year/member).** All other per-check costs are user-paid passthrough + $5.
- **Vendor pricing changes flow through automatically.** When Checkr or Plaid changes their rates, Werkles' user-facing price updates with them. Margin stays $5.

Shared workspace each Blueprint team gets after Lock-the-Joints mutual accept. Role clarity, document checklist, milestones, blueprint membership, task ownership.

| Access | Price | Status |
|---|---:|---|

## 5. Sponsored Anvils - B2B Revenue

Disclosed promotional placements and affiliate partnerships. Vendor pays Werkles. Werkles never takes a cut of user-to-user deals.

### 5.1 Status: Principles LOCKED, Specific Rates DEFERRED

Specific rates wait until Operator has a launch metro chosen and actual vendors to negotiate with. Principles below are Constitutional law and apply to every Sponsored Anvil agreement.

### 5.2 Principles (locked)

- **FTC disclosure required on every placement.** "Sponsored" or "Promoted" label visible.
- **No exclusive sponsorship deals.** Members are always told that competing alternatives exist.
- **Infrastructure vendors (Stripe Identity, Plaid, Checkr, Twilio, PostHog, Expo Push) are never Sponsored Anvils.** They are paid services Werkles uses; their visibility in product must come from product fit, not sponsor revenue.
- **Werkles never takes a percentage of user-to-user deals.** Affiliate revenue must always be paid by the vendor, never deducted from the member. Member sees a discount; Werkles invoices the vendor.
- **No revenue share with venues on commerce at meet-ups.** Flat fees only.

## 6. Explicitly Out Of Scope

The following are pre-rejected. They break the locked Constitutional posture and must never be added to this document:

- Tiered membership (Pro, Enterprise, "Founder Lifetime" beyond the marketing layer)
- Pay-for-visibility on profiles
- Pay-for-better-matches
- Take rate on user-to-user deals when they close
- Carry interest on Werkles-introduced deals
- Pay-per-introduction / Thumbtack-style lead fees
- Werkles-branded loans
- Werkles-held escrow for user-to-user transactions
- "Premium verification" where Werkles charges more for stronger badges
- Cap table tooling on platform

Each is rejected with a specific compliance reason documented in the Compliance One-Pager draft and the Constitution Iron Firewall.
