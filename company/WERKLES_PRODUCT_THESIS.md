# Werkles Product Thesis

Status: v0.2 review draft

## Article IV - What Werkles Is

Werkles is a private partner-discovery and verification-gated networking platform for serious business builders, operators, backers, connectors, workers, and sparks.

Werkles helps users:

- create a dossier
- state their lane, arena, turf, skills, needs, and proof posture
- browse possible fit
- understand why a match appears
- request an intro
- build or join Blueprints
- take conversations off-platform with their own advisers

Werkles is centered on the Multi-Member Blueprint Pattern. The Blueprint is the room. Members can join many-to-many through `blueprint_members` or its successor table. The venture, job, shop, route, property, or project is the center, not a single hero user.

## What Werkles Is Not

Werkles is not:

- a bank
- a lender
- a broker
- a securities marketplace
- an investment adviser
- an escrow provider
- a law firm
- an accounting firm
- an employer
- a labor-organizing command center
- a public social network for business theater

Werkles never holds, moves, or escrows money between users. The deal is theirs to make.

## Data Integrity And Deletion

To protect Blueprint integrity, bad actors may be quarantined through account status rather than immediately erased from relational history.

However, users who exercise deletion rights under CCPA, GDPR, or equivalent state law must be handled through the Privacy Policy deletion/anonymization process. When hard deletion is required, PII and verification artifacts must be removed or anonymized while preserving non-identifying Blueprint structure where legally allowed.

Implementation details remain open and must be resolved before launch.
