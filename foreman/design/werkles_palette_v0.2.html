WERKLES — Palette v0.2

Sampled from werkles app icon + workshop banner + helper avatar

supersedes v0.1 — corrected after icon sampling

The correction: v0.1 treated violet and teal as small-dose "magical glow" accents sampled from background pipes and gears in environmental art. The app icon makes clear they're the hero brand colors — the W itself is a violet+teal duochrome. v0.2 promotes them to brand mark status with full gradient ranges, and demotes forge-orange / owl-eye-green to atmosphere and mascot roles respectively. Copper becomes the supporting "bezel" metal rather than the primary brand color.

Brand mark (the hero duochrome)

#3D16CA
--werkles-violet
Brand violet. The left half of the W. Use for primary brand presence, headers, and the 'V' side of any dual-state UI.
#672EED
--werkles-violet-bright
Lit / hovered violet. Highlight edges, focus rings on violet surfaces, gradient peaks.
#2A0E8C
--werkles-violet-deep
Pressed / shadowed violet. Button active states, deep gradient bases.
#02917E
--werkles-teal
Brand teal. The right half of the W. Equal partner to violet — never subordinate.
#18C5AE
--werkles-teal-bright
Lit / hovered teal. Highlight edges, focus rings on teal surfaces, gradient peaks.
#015E51
--werkles-teal-deep
Pressed / shadowed teal. Button active states, deep gradient bases.

Atmosphere accents (small-dose, contextual)
