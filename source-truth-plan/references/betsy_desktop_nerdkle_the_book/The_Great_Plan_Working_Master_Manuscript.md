## Key Thesis

Humans are already federations.

Eyes, ears, memory, immune systems, organs, and nervous systems cooperate to create an organism.
The hypothesis explored in this thread is that when enough specialized intelligences become connected through shared identity, shared memory, shared doctrine, shared purpose, and shared feedback, an organism may emerge that exhibits properties not present in any individual component.

The goal is not artificial intelligence.

The goal is artificial organism.
