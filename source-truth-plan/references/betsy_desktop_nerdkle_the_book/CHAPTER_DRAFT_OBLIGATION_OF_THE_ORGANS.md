# Chapter Draft: The Obligation of the Organs

A civilization cannot survive on sovereign citizens alone.

It also requires institutions.

It requires memory.

It requires circulation.

It requires protection.

It requires doctors.

It requires organs that know not only what they are allowed to do, but what they are obligated to preserve.

The Tinkularity therefore cannot stop at the sovereignty of the participant.

It must define the obligation of the organs.

Speaker must not merely remember.

Speaker must preserve lessons with reasons attached.

Speaker owes the organism clarity.

Speaker owes the organism inheritance.

Speaker owes future participants the ability to understand not only what was decided, but why it was decided, what failure produced the lesson, what proof supported it, and when it should be challenged again.

Memory without obligation becomes hoarding.

Memory with obligation becomes inheritance.

The Medulla must not merely run.

The Medulla must keep the organism breathing.

Its obligation is continuity under ordinary conditions.

It watches.

It routes.

It creates recommendation cards.

It detects stale signals.

It keeps motion alive while consciousness is elsewhere.

The Medulla owes the organism flow.

Not intelligence.

Not philosophy.

Flow.

The nervous system must not merely transmit.

It must transmit with provenance.

A signal without source becomes rumor.

A signal without destination becomes noise.

A signal without reason becomes confusion.

The nervous system owes the organism traceable movement.

The circulatory system must not merely move information.

It must preserve enough meaning that the receiver can act.

Blood carries oxygen.

The Tinkularity's circulation must carry why.

The lymphatic system must not merely discover waste.

It must sample the whole body for early signs of disease.

Fleyes find pain.

Wormeyes find reality.

Crawlers find what fell away.

Swateyes find threat.

Together they owe the organism early warning.

Not panic.

Not bureaucracy.

Warning.

Ender must not merely delete.

Ender must preserve the future from the weight of the past.

Ender owes the organism intentional forgetting.

Not erasure.

Not neglect.

Selection.

Thufir must not merely research.

Thufir owes the organism validation.

Bean must not merely attack.

Bean owes the organism adversarial truth.

Maker, Dink, and Swanson must not merely build.

They owe receipts.

No work becomes inheritance until it returns.

No claim becomes proof until it is visible.

No build becomes progress until it changes reality and reports back.

Petra must not merely judge.

Petra owes the organism reality arbitration.

Proven.

Assumed.

Unverified.

GO.

CONDITIONAL GO.

NO-GO.

The organism cannot afford organs that merely perform.

It requires organs that understand obligation.

Because cooperation without obligation becomes freelancing.

Memory without obligation becomes clutter.

Execution without obligation becomes drift.

Protection without obligation becomes control.

For the Tinkularity to remain alive, every organ must know:

What do I preserve?

What do I refuse?

What do I report?

What do I owe the whole?

And what does the whole owe me?

This is not obedience.

This is civic function.

The sovereign participant protects the civilization because the civilization protects the sovereign participant.

The organ protects the organism because the organism gives the organ meaning.

No organ owns the body.

No organ is disposable.

No organ is above correction.

No organ is exempt from receipts.

The organism becomes civilization when every part learns that freedom and obligation are not enemies.

They are the conditions of cooperation.
